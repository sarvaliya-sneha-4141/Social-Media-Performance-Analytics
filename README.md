# 📊 Meta Ad Performance Dashboard – Social Media Performance Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Meta Ad Performance Dashboard** is an interactive analytics report built using **Microsoft Power BI** to monitor and evaluate paid social media campaign performance across Meta platforms.

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of impressions, clicks, conversions, engagement metrics, budget utilization, and audience insights. It enables marketers to track campaign effectiveness, optimize ad spend, and make data-driven advertising decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

* Monitor overall advertising performance
* Analyze engagement trends across demographics
* Track conversion efficiency and purchase behavior
* Evaluate budget allocation and campaign ROI
* Identify top-performing ad types and audience segments

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (Top Summary Cards)

The dashboard displays high-level KPIs for quick performance monitoring:

* **216K Impressions** – Total ad views
* **25.4K Clicks** – Total user interactions
* **1.3K Shares**
* **2.6K Comments**
* **1.3K Purchases**
* **29.3K Engagements**
* **11.76% CTR (Click-Through Rate)**
* **13.56% Engagement Rate**
* **5.21% Conversion Rate**
* **0.61% Purchase Rate**
* **$2.5M Total Budget**
* **$50.7K Average Budget per Campaign**

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of campaign efficiency and customer interaction levels.

---

### 👥 Audience Insights

#### Engagement by Gender

* Female audience contributes the highest engagement share (~43%)
* Male and other segments follow
  This helps in refining audience targeting strategies.

#### Engagement by Age

* Strong engagement observed in the 20–30 age group
* Gradual decline in engagement after age 35
  This supports age-based campaign optimization.

#### Engagement by Country

Geographical performance is visualized using a map to identify high-performing regions and optimize regional ad spend.

---

### 📈 Trend Analysis

#### Weekly Engagement Trend

Stacked bar visualization shows daily engagement distribution across the week, helping identify peak activity periods.

#### Hourly Engagement Trend

Line chart representation highlights user activity by hour, useful for:

* Determining best posting times
* Optimizing ad scheduling

---

### 📅 Monthly & Calendar Analysis

The dashboard includes:

  This helps track seasonality and campaign timing impact.
